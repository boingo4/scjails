About Our Training Network

SC Jails Training Group coordinates STC-certified Type 1 jail training, regional provider resources, and quarterly jail manager meetings for Southern California. Members use this site to find course calendars, approved providers, facility directories, legal links, and real-time updates for correctional training requirements in this region.

Whether you are a jail manager, training officer, or corrections provider in Southern California, this site helps keep your team compliant and connected with the latest local Type 1 jail training information.
